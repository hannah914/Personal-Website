As part of my honors project for Lakeland I am working on creating a program to help students with their resume. This would give students the ability to quickly check their resume compared to a job description. The program would look to make sure the resume has strong language choices like beginning bullet points with action words. Along with this it will check for basic grammar and spelling errors.
